## 1 Peter Questions

### 1:1
* Why is Peter writing specifically to the pilgrims of the Dispersion?

### 1:2
* What is meant by "elect according to the foreknowledge of God"?
* Why is the sprinkling of the blood of Jesus mentioned?
* What is the significance of the series of statements regarding the elect?
* Why does he say "...peace be multiplied" instead of "grace and peace" as Paul often states?
* What does it mean for peace to be multiplied?

### 1:3
* What is their living hope?
* Why does Peter alternate from the usage of "us" to "you" in verses 3 and 4

### 1:5
* If God is the one keeping them, what is the significance of it being through faith?

### 1:7
* Is there a difference between the praise brought to Jesus immediately and the praise brought when He returns? If so, what is it?
