# Bible Chapter Summaries

## Genesis

### Genesis 1  short summary - CREATION

God spoke; there was light and darkness, there was firmament and water, there was Earth, Seas, grass, herb and fruit, there was lights for seasons, two great lights and stars, there was creatures in the water and birds in the air, there was animals, and man. And God called it all good.

### Genesis 2 short summary - GARDEN

God rested the seventh day. Thus God created the heavens and the earth. And man was made from dust and breathed to life by God. God planted a garden and put man there. There was a tree of life and a tree of the knowledge of good and evil. There was rivers. God commanded man not to eat of the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. Adam named the animals. Woman was formed from mans rib.

### Genesis 3 short summary - REBELLION

The serpant questions and twists Gods word about the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. The woman and her husband eat of it. They hear the Lord God walking in the garden and hide from Him. God questions their actions. The serpant is cursed, the womans sorrow and conception is greatly multiplied. The ground is cursed for mans sake. God makes tunics of skin for Adam and his wife. God sends them out of the garden lest they eat of the tree of life. God places cherubim and a flaming sword in the garden.

### Genesis 4 short summary - MURDER

Cain and Abel are born. Cain offers from the ground, Abel offers the firstborn of his flock. The Lord speaks to Cain. Cain kills Abel. The Lord questions Cain and Cain is cursed from the earth for his action. The Lord sets a mark on Cain. Cain builds a city and names it after his son, Enoch. There is generations from Enoch. Lamech speaks to his wives. Adam has another son. Then men began to call on the name of the Lord.

### Genesis 5 short summary - GENEALOGY

This is the book of the genealogy of Adam. God created man in his likeness. He created them male and female. Enoch walked with God. Lamech begot Noah.

### Genesis 6 short summary - CORRUPTION

Men began to multiply on the earth. The daughters of men were beautiful. Mens days shall be one hundred and twenty years. There were mighty men of renown. The wickedness of man was great in the earth. The Lord said he will destroy all from the face of the earth, for He was sorry He created them. Noah found grace in the eyes of the Lord. Noah walked with God. The earth was corrupt and filled with violence. God speaks to Noah and commands him to build an ark. God says He Himself will bring floodwaters to destroy everything that is on the earth. Naoh is commanded to bring two of every living thing into the ark, male and female. Noah did according to all that God commanded him.

### Genesis 7 short summary - ARK

The Lord says come into the ark. Male and female animals and birds are taken into the ark, to keep the species alive on the face of all the earth. Noah did according to all that the Lord commanded him. The fountains of the deep were broken up, and the windows of heaven were opened. Noah, his sons, his wife, the three wives of his sons, every beast, cattle, creeping thing, and bird after it's kind entered the ark. The waters prevailed and greatly increased on the earth. All flesh died that moved on the earth. Only Noah and those who were with him in the ark remained alive.

### Genesis 8 short summary - RECEDED

God remembered Noah and all who were with him in the ark. The waters receded and the ark rested on the mountains of Ararat. Noah sent out a raven and a dove. The second time the dove returns with an olive leaf. The waters dried up from the earth. God speaks to Noah and tells him and every living thing to go out of the ark be fruitful and multiply on the earth. Noah builds an altar to the Lord. The Lord smelled a soothing aroma and speaks in His heart.

### Genesis 9 short summary - RAINBOW

God blessed Noah and his sons. Every living thing shall fear man and are given into mans hand and are for food. All things were given to man, even as the green herbs. God commands to not eat flesh with its life, that is, it's blood. For God will demand a reckoning. God establishes His covenant with Noah and his descendants after him and with every living creature. The sign of the covenant is a rainbow seen in the cloud. Noahs sons who were in the ark were, Shem, Ham, and Japheth. Noah planted a vineyard and becomes drunk from the wine. Noah speaks of his sons.

### Genesis 10 short summary - DISPERSED

This is the genealogy of the sons of Noah. The coastland peoples of the Gentiles were separated into their lands. Nimrod was a mighty hunter before the Lord. The Canaanites were dispersed. In the days of Peleg the earth was divided.

### Genesis 11 short summary - BABEL

The whole earth had one language and one speech. They said: "Come let us build ourselves a city, and a tower whose top is in the heavens; let us make a name for ourselves, lest we be scattered abroad over the face of the whole earth." The Lord scattered them abroad from there over the face of all the earth, and they ceased building the city. The genealogy of Shem. The genealogy of Terah. Abram's wife Sarai was barren. Terah took his son Abram and grandson Lot from Ur of the Chaldeans to go to the land of Canaan. Terah died in Haran.

### Genesis 12 short summary - BLESSING

Abram will be blessed and be a blessing. Abram and Lot depart from Haran, to the land of Canaan, as far as the terebinth tree of Moreh, the Lord appeared and said to Abram, "To your descendants I will give this land". Abram built an alter to the Lord. There was severe famine. The Egyptians saw that Sarai was very beautiful. The Lord plagued Pharaoh and his house because of Sarai. Pharaoh questions Abram then sends him away with his wife and all that he had.

### Genesis 13 short summary -

Abram was very rich in livestock, in silver and in gold. Abram called on the name of the Lord. Abram and Lot's possessions were so great that they could not dwell together. They separated from each other. Abram dwelt in the land of Canaan, and Lot dwelt in the cities of the plain. God said to Abram that he will make Abram's descendants as the dust of the earth and gave the land to him. Then Abram moved his tent and went and dwelt by the terebinth trees of Mamre.

### Genesis 14 short summary -

The kings made war with kings. They joined together in battle in the valley of Siddim, there were asphalt pits. Sodom and Gomorrah fled; some fell there, the remainder fled to the mountains. Lot was taken who dwelt in Sodom. Abram heard his brother was taken captive, armed his servants and went in pursuit as far as Dan. So he brought back everything that was taken.  Melchizedek blesses Abram.

### Genesis 15 short summary -

The Lord came to Abram in a vision saying, "Do not be afraid, Abram. I am your shield, your exceeding great reward". God says Abram will have descendants numbered as many as the stars. Abram believed in the Lord, and He accounted it to him for righteousness. The Lord tells Abram that his descendants will be strangers in the land and serve a nation whom the Lord will judge. On the same day the Lord made a covenant with Abram, saying: "To your descendants I have given this land, ..."

### Genesis 16 short summary -

Sarai, Abram's wife, had borne him no children. Sarai dealt harshly with Hagar. An angel of the Lord speaks to Hagar by a spring of water on the way to Shur. Hagar bore Abram a son, and Abram named his son, whom Hagar bore, Ishmael.

### Genesis 17 short summary -

When Abram was ninety-nine years old, the Lord appeared to Abram and said to him, “I am Almighty God; walk before Me and be blameless. And I will make My covenant between Me and you, and will multiply you exceedingly.” Then Abram fell on his face and God talked to him... “No longer shall your name be called Abram, but your name shall be Abraham...” ... “And I will establish My covenant between Me and you and your descendants after you in their generations, for an everlasting covenant, to be God to you and your descendants after you.” ... “As for Sarai your wife, you shall not call her name Sarai, but Sarah shall be her name” ... “...Sarah your wife shall bear you a son, and you shall call his name Isaac...”

### Genesis 18 short summary -

Then the Lord appeared to Abraham by the Terebinth trees of Mamre as he was sitting in the tent door in the heat of the day. Three men visit Abraham. Sarah will have a son. Abraham speaks with the Lord.

### Genesis 19 short summary -

Two angels came to Sodom in the evening. Lot insisted strongly that they stay at his house. Lot spoke to the men of the city at his doorway. The angels urged Lot to hurry and take his family away lest they be consumed in the punishment of the city. Lot escaped to Zoar and the Lord destroyed Sodom and Gomorrah with fire and brimstone from the heavens. Lot went up from Zoar and dwelt in the mountains.

### Genesis 20 short summary -

Abraham journeyed from there to the south and dwelt between Kadesh and Shur, and stayed in Gerar. Abraham said Sarah was his sister and the king of Gerar took Sarah. God came to Abimelech in a dream by night. Abimelech questioned Abraham. Abimelech restored Sarah to Abraham.

### Genesis 21 short summary -

Sarah bore Abraham a son in his old age, at the set time of which God had spoken to him. Sarah said cast out the bondwoman. God said to Abraham in Isaac your seed shall be called. Hagar departs and wondered in the Wilderness of Beersheba and an angel of God call to Hagar out of heaven.
