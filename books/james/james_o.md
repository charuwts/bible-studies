## James Observations
### 1:1
* James is a bondservant of God
* This letter is to the twelve tribes
  * They are scattered abroad

### 1:2-4
* Immediate command
* They are to count it joy when the fall into trials
  * testing of faith produces patience
  * Patience has a perfect work
    * They are to let it happen so that they may be perfect
    * lacking nothing
