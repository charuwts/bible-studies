### Q&A

* __How did “all the world” become guilty before God?:__
  * By the Law which brought the the knowledge of sin.
  * (Romans 3:19,20)
* __What is man’s state in light of his guilt?:__
  * Afar off, condemned, separated and enemies of God.
  * (Job 9:19-20, Isaiah 59:2, Ephesians 2:13-17, James 4:4, Matthew 7:23, Matthew 25:41, Luke 13:27, Colossians 1:21)

  * No, all our righteousness is as filthy rags
* __What is man’s need?:__
  * A savior, to be made righteous, that we be brought back into fellowship with God

## 7. The Atonement - The Precious Blood of Christ

### Passage Summaries


* " 1 Peter 18-21
* = We are redeemed by the precious blood of Christ
* " Ephesians 2:13
* = We have been brought near
* " Hebrews 9:14
* = our conscience is cleansed from dead works to serve the living God
* " 1 John 1:7
* = the blood of Jesus Christ cleanses us from all sin
* " Revelation 1:5
* = He washed us from our sins in His own blood

### Q&A



What was the Precious Blood of Christ for?

1. for atonement - to pacify, propitiate, reconcile (Romans 5:11)
2. for propitiation - a just and satisfying offering in our stead (Romans 3:25)
3. for our justification from sin (Romans 5:9)
4. for the forgiveness of sins (Ephesians 1:7, Colossians 1:14, Hebrews 9:7)
5. for the remission of sins . (Matthew 26:28, Romans 3:25, Hebrews 9:22)
6. for the cleansing, washing from all sin (1 John 1:7, Revelation 1:5, Revelation 7:14)
7. for the purging of our consciences (Hebrews 9:14)
8. for peace (Colossians 1:20)
9. for reconciliation unto Christ (Colossians 1:20)
10. for righteousness (Romans 3:22, 2 Corinthians 5:21, Philippians 3:9)
11. for the purpose of saving us from the wrath that will come (Romans 5:9)
12. for the destruction of the devil (Hebrews 2:14)
13. for overcoming the Devil (Revelation 12:11)
14. for redemption , eternal redemption - for the purchase of our very beings (Ephesians 1:7, Colossians 1:14, Hebrews 9:12, Revelation 5:9, Acts 20:28)
15. for the purpose of giving us life within, eternal life (John 6:53,54)
16. for the bringing back to life (Hebrews 13:20)
17. for sanctification (Hebrews 10:29; Hebrews 13:12)
18. for spiritual and physical healing (Isaiah 53:5; 1 Peter 2:24)
19. for boldness to enter in to the Holy of Holies , the very presence of God (Hebrews 10:19)
20. for the purpose of enabling us to make our daily, hourly, minute-by-minute Christ Jesus (John 6:56)
