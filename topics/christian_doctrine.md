# Christian Doctrine

## Essential Points

- Soteriology: The doctrine of Savlation
- Hamartiology: The doctrine of Sin
- Bibliology: What the Bible is and why it matters
- Theology Proper: The being, attributes and works of God
  - Pneumatology: The study of the Holy Spirit
  - Christology: The nature and work of Jesus Christ

## Finer Points

- Eschatology: The study of the end times; The return of Christ
